Bayesian Task

We invite you to participate in a research study conducted by investigators from Washington University in St. Louis. The purpose of the study is to evaluate how different representations of Bayesian data impact cognitive load.

Instructions

  • In this HIT, you will complete a reasoning task followed by a cognitive reflection test and a spatial ability test.
  • You will be presented with a Bayesian problem. Read the prompt carefully and once you are ready, click on the button to answer two questions about the prompt
  • Once you are confident about your answers, click on the Submit button.
  • Next, we will conduct a Cognitive Relection Test consisting of 3 questions
  • Finally, we will conduct a Spatial Ability Test
  • Then you will be prompted for some basic demographic information questions (age, gender, and education).
  • The average completion time is 20 minutes
  • Payment
    • You will receive a base pay of $1.00 for your participation
    • Additional Bonus: We will determine your bonus based on how you perform on the task. You can win a total bonus of up to $3.25.

Terms of Service

  • Identifying information will never be used or shared. Your MTurk ID will be replaced with a random alphanumeric code.
  • Taking part in this research study is completely voluntary. You may choose not to take part at all. If you decide to be in this study, you may stop participating at any time. However, you may only get paid after finishing all the questions. Addditional, any data that was collected will remain as part of the study records and cannot be removed.
