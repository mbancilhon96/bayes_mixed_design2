Workload & Reasoning Study

We invite you to participate in a research study conducted by investigators from Washington University in St. Louis. The purpose of the study is to evaluate how different representations of Bayesian data impact cognitive load.

Instructions

  • Please set your browser to full screen and stay on the page until the end of the study.

  • In this HIT, you will complete two Statistical Reasoning tasks. In each task, you will estimate a true positive rate based on the data provided. For one of those two tasks, you will need to remember a dot pattern while you complete it, and reproduce it after the task. After each Statistical Reasoning task, you will be asked about the effort you exerted.
  • Finally, we will conduct a Spatial Ability Test and a short demographic questionnaire.
  • The average completion time is 20 minutes
  • Payment
    • You will receive a base pay of $2.00 for your participation
    • Additional Bonus: We will determine your bonus based on how you perform on the task. You can win a total bonus of up to $2.25.

    Terms of Service

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information. Your MTurk ID will be replaced with a random alphanumeric code.
  • Taking part in this research study is completely voluntary. You may choose not to take part at all. If you decide to be in this study, you may stop participating at any time. However, you may only get paid after finishing all the questions. Additionally, any data that was collected will remain as part of the study records and cannot be removed.
